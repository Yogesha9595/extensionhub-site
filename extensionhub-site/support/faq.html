FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I install an extension?
Open the extension page and click “Install” to go to the official store listing (or provider link).
Do extensions collect my browsing data?
We aim for privacy-first tools. Each extension page includes a “Why you can trust this extension” section. Always review permissions and policies before installing.
Where can I request a refund?
Review our Refund Policy, then email billing@extensionhub.in with your purchase details.
How do I contact support?
Email support@extensionhub.in or use Contact.
Do you use affiliate links?
Sometimes, yes. Affiliate links never change the price you pay. We disclose this in the footer and on pages.
