Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 Jan 2026

ExtensionHub (“we”, “us”) builds browser extensions and web pages. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Support emails: If you contact us, we receive your email address and message content.
  • Website analytics (optional): If enabled, we may collect basic anonymous traffic metrics.
  • Payments: If you purchase, payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store your full card details.

How we use information

  • To provide support and respond to queries.
  • To operate and improve our products.
  • To prevent fraud and abuse.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with service providers only as needed to operate our services (for example, payment processing via Stripe).

Data retention

We retain support communications for as long as necessary to provide support, comply with legal obligations, and improve our services.

Your choices

  • You can request deletion of support conversations by emailing support@extensionhub.in.
  • You can uninstall our extensions anytime from your browser.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@extensionhub.in
